When does a local entity start to make sense?
Most global HR advisors put the crossover point at 8–15 employees in-country, once the recurring cost of EOR per head exceeds the annualized cost of running your own entity (legal, accounting, registered agent, and HR overhead). If SyBridge plans to cap India hiring at ~7 people, EOR is very likely the right permanent structure — not just an interim step.
